Alankaran

Project Pradan was initiated in 2016. This project aims to provide essential resources to the underprivileged children as per the requirements. These resources ranges from providing nutritious food items to medicines, from clothes to sanitary napkins. Stationary kits are also distributed among the students. More than 400 children have benefited from this project till now
