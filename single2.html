FEED INDIA

“Give you hands to serve and your hearts to love”

With this though, Under the feed india vertical, we have our new project Aahar. Project Aahar aims to provide nutritious and healthy meals to the needy one’s.

The food for the project Aahar is prepared by the students from the institute of Hotel Management, Bhopal in a hygienic location using high-quality ingredients.

The donation money from the people help us to buy the materials required for cooking nutritious and healthy meals

It’s not the amount that matters but the meaning behind your donation.Giving a little is better than not giving at all.

Project Aahar aims to feed fifty people every week, with distribution drives going every weekend.
